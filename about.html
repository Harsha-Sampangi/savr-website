About Savr

Our Mission

Savr was founded in 2023 with a simple goal: to help Indian consumers save money and time on their everyday purchases. In a market flooded with multiple delivery and transportation options, making the right choice can be overwhelming.

We aggregate real-time data from India's top services to provide instant price and delivery time comparisons at your fingertips.

Why Choose Savr?

Unlike other comparison tools, Savr specializes in the Indian market, understanding local pricing structures, delivery networks, and regional variations that affect both grocery delivery and cab services.

Our algorithms take into account not just the base prices but also delivery fees, surge pricing, discounts, and loyalty programs to give you the true cost of your purchase or ride.

The Team

Savr is built by a team of data scientists, developers, and consumer advocates who are passionate about creating tools that empower consumers in the digital economy.
